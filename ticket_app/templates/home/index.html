A new, simple, way to book flight delay insurance

Just book insurance and forget it. You get paid automatically if your flight is delayed

This is how it works

1. You enter a flight ID of a German flight and date of the flight.

2. The platform predicts the flight delay.

3. Based on the prediction your payout rates are decided for different length of delays. If the delays are predicted, the insurance payout will be lesser. If flight is predicted to be on-time, payout will be higher.

4. On creating the insurance, you will receive a blockchain receipt of your data.

5. Based on the actual delay of the flight selected, you will be payed automatically.

Historical flight data analysis

For getting the most accurate flight delays for Germany, a huge amount of flight data was collected. A dataset of over 400,000 flights over last six months, including the weather conditions at all German airports.

Machine Learning

The dataset was used to predict future flight delays. Based on the prediction, insurance payour rates are decided. A weighted random forest model was selected for the most accurate delay prediction.

Blockchain

A secure hash of your data is stored in the Bitcoin blockchain, to ensure your Insurance Agreement was created. The blockchain receipt is provided to you to verify it via any third party too.

Hassle free claims

The claims process has been completely removed from the platform. If your flight gets delayed, you will get payed automatically based on how long your flight was delayed.
